Acid rain slicked the grimy alley, reflecting the neon glow of UrukCorp’s towering spires.  Gilgamesh’s back pressed against cold, crumbling concrete. The air buzzed, thick with the whine of approaching drones. His cranial implant throbbed, a searing pulse of red light painting the inside of his eyelids. *Target reacquired. Elimination protocol initiated.*  

He pushed himself up, the tattered remnants of his regal coat clinging to his armored frame.  His data-reticle eye flickered, analyzing the swarm – six UrukCorp Hunter-Killers, standard issue. "King? I can't even rule my own bloodstream anymore," he muttered, a sardonic grin twisting his lips.
