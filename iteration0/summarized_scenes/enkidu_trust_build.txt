In a decaying data-shrine, Enkidu experiences fragmented memories triggered by the environment.  Gilgamesh, displaying increasing coldness, dismisses Enkidu's feelings as mere data remnants.  Enkidu confronts Gilgamesh about his changing demeanor, perceiving him as shedding a former identity.  Gilgamesh claims he is merely remembering who he is, increasing the tension between them.  Enkidu pleads with Gilgamesh to prove he's still the same person, reaching out to him.
