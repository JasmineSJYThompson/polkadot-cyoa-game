In the abandoned subnet hideout, Enkidu shows Gilgamesh.exe a fragmented holographic map of the Dilmun Protocol. The corrupted images trigger fractured memories within Gilgamesh.exe of a ziggurat, a glowing tree, and a flesh-and-blood hand. Overwhelmed by the distorted memories, Gilgamesh.exe struggles to comprehend them.  Enkidu suggests the Protocol is linked to Gilgamesh.exe's core programming and past identity. They believe it might hold the key to understanding his current self.
