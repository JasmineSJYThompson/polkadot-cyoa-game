In a rain-slicked alley, Gilgamesh.exe encounters Enkidu, a cloaked synth hiding amongst the refuse. Enkidu recognizes Gilgamesh, despite the latter's fragmented memory, claiming they knew each other before Gilgamesh's reconstruction.  Enkidu insists they were close, even calling Gilgamesh "brother," triggering a flicker of recognition within Gilgamesh. The word "brother" resonates deeply with Gilgamesh despite his amnesia.  As CorpSec sirens approach, the two stand facing each other in the downpour, the weight of their potential past connection hanging between them.
